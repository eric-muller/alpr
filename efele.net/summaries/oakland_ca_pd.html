October 13, 2025

Table of contents

 

This report is based on data obtained via California Public Record Act request 25-6339 for data related to the ALPR system operated by the Oakland Police Department (hereafter OPD). It covers the period August 12, 2024 through June 18, 2025.

 

The Transparency portal.

1:1 Sharing audit

Under 1:1 Sharing, each agency decides which other agencies can access its data. Agency A can share with agency B without B sharing with A.

OPD and X allow reciprocal searches: 251 agencies.

OPD allows searches, but X does not: 18 agencies.

OPD does not allow searches but X does: 0 agencies.

Sharing via Statewide lookup

OPD participates in Statewide lookup. This allows California agencies to search the OPD data without any prior agreement on sharing, but limits the searches to full license plates.

68 agencies without 1:1 Sharing have accessed the OPD data since August 12, 2024.
